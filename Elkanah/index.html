Seoul - South Korea

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a vibrant metropolis that seamlessly blends ancient traditions with cutting-edge technology. From the historic Gyeongbokgung Palace to the bustling streets of Myeongdong, Seoul offers a unique cultural experience. The city is renowned for its delicious cuisine, including dishes like kimchi and bibimbap, as well as its dynamic K-pop music scene. With its efficient public transportation system and numerous parks and recreational areas, Seoul is a city that caters to both residents and visitors alike.
